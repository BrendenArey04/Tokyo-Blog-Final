Topic Research — Tokyo (finalized)

This research collects high-quality sources, summarises their findings, and explains how they support the content on this site.

  1. Japan National Tourism Organization (JNTO) — official travel information, seasonal events, and cultural guides. Useful for accurate dates and official visitor resources. (japan.travel)
  2. Tokyo Metropolitan Government — local government site with city guides, safety information, and event calendars. Useful for logistics and official notices. (metro.tokyo.lg.jp)
  3. Travel blogs & food journalism — Ichiran, Tsukiji, Harajuku reports and reviews (used to identify popular food spots & local tips).
  4. Academic / cultural resources — articles on hanami and cultural etiquette that informed the 'Culture' and 'Key Concepts' sections.

How the research was used

The information above was used to create accurate, well-structured content across pages (food, places, and cultural practices). Each resource is cited above and was checked for reliability.

Key places & cultural terms in Tokyo
Term Meaning / Why it matters
Shibuya Major shopping and entertainment district — home to the Scramble Crossing.
Akihabara Electronics and otaku culture center (anime, games, maid cafés).
Hanami Cherry blossom viewing parties in spring — a major cultural event.
Tsukiji Famous fish market (Outer Market remains a popular food destination).
